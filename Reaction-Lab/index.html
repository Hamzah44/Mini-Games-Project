🧪 How to Play
1
Pick 2 Ingredients
Tap any two chemicals from the shelf below the beaker. They'll load into the mixing slots.
2
Hit ⚡ REACT!
Press the green REACT button to mix them. Watch the beaker and see what happens — explosions, flames, colour changes and more!
3
Discover All 20 Reactions
Every valid combo triggers a real chemical or physical reaction with a science fact and equation. Find all 33 to become a Master Chemist!
4
Earn XP & Level Up
New discoveries give bonus XP. Build streaks for multipliers. Complete challenges for huge rewards.
💡
Stuck?
Use the Hint Lab to get tiered chemistry clues for undiscovered reactions.

Journal

🔥
Streaks
Chain successful reactions for XP multipliers. Dumping the beaker breaks your streak!
🎯
Challenges
Bonus goals like "discover all flame tests" or "build a 5× streak" for extra XP.
